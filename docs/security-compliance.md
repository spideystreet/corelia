## Regulatory Framework

### EU AI Act Compliance
- **Risk Level**: High Risk (medical administrative decisions)
- **Requirements**: Risk management, data governance, technical documentation, human oversight
- **Status**: Framework defined, implementation planned

### GDPR & CNIL Compliance
- **Data Protection**: Privacy by design principles
- **French Regulations**: CNIL compliance for medical data processing
- **Anonymization**: Patient data protection with medical entity preservation
- **Status**: Compliance roadmap established

## Infrastructure Security

### OVH SecNumCloud (Target)
- **Certification**: ANSSI Visa de Sécurité
- **Sovereignty**: French-controlled infrastructure
- **Healthcare**: HDS (Hébergeur de Données de Santé) compatible
- **Data Localization**: Guaranteed French territory residency
